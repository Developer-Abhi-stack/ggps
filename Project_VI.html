GANGA GLOBAL PUBLIC SCHOOL provides the best education to children. The purpose is to nurture the unique potential of the students entrusted to our care. We aim to offer a liberal and enlightened education environment that keeps pace with dynamic trends in an eco-friendly, pollution-free, and well-built campus equipped with the latest facilities.

About Us



GGPS was established in Dumrama, Amarpur, in February 2024. Its mission is to nurture ideal citizens who will serve the country and contribute to humanity's betterment. The school's main campus is located in Dumrama, approximately 1 kilometer from Amarpur, on the Amarpur-Bhagalpur Road. The campus features a large playground, modern science and computer labs with broadband internet, a well-stocked library, and a fully furnished hostel with 24-hour power backup and RO-purified drinking water.
